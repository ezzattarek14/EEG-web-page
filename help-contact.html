FAQ

Common questions from users, partners, and clinics.

How does the system work?

The headset captures signal patterns, the AI model interprets intent, and the arm executes movement. The mobile app calibrates and improves control over time.

How do I choose sizes for ordering?

Use the Order page to select head size, arm size, hand size, and arm side (right/left). For best accuracy, follow clinician measurements.

How do I download the app?

On the Products page, enter your device serial number to unlock download links.
