HOW IT WORKS

From first use to stable control

The workflow is designed to be clear and repeatable for users, clinics, and partners.

Step 1

Setup & pairing

The headset pairs with the system and prepares signals for interpretation. The app assists with guided setup.

Step 2

Calibration session

A short calibration maps your unique signals to control commands. This improves accuracy and reduces confusion.

Step 3

Training & improvement

Training sessions refine performance over time. The system adapts to the user for smoother, more reliable control.
