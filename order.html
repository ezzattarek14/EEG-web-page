How to choose sizes

This section explains what the size fields mean so the customer selects correctly.

Head size: headset fit and comfort. Choose based on head circumference.

Arm size: prosthetic structure length and socket compatibility. Choose based on your measurements or clinician guidance.

Hand size: grip geometry and comfort. Choose based on palm width and finger length preference.

Arm side: select Right or Left depending on which arm is needed.
